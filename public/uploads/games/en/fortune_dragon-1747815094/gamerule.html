Fortune Dragon
Fortune Dragon is a 3-reel, 3-row video slot featuring multiplier reel up to x10 and Fortune Spins with expanded multiplier reel.
The game is played with {BaseBet} bet lines (fixed), bet level of {LevelStart} to {LevelEnd} and bet size of {BetSizeStart} to {BetSizeEnd}.
The in-game currency is {CurrencyPrefix}.
The Bet Size is set using the "Bet Size" selector.
The Bet Level is set using the "Bet Level" selector.
The Bet Amount is set using the "Bet Amount" selector.
"Cash Wallet" displays the cash available for wager.
"Auto Spin" automatically plays the game for a selected number of rounds.
Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the "Paytable".
A bet line win in cash is equal to the value shown in the "Paytable" multiplied by the bet size and bet level.
Only the highest win per bet line is paid.
Bet lines win if the winning symbols are in succession from the leftmost reel to the right.
Simultaneous wins on different bet lines are added.
All wins shown in cash.
Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols.
Multiplier Reel
During any spin, the multiplier reel at the bottom of the reels will spin to reveal either blank, x2, x5 or x10.
If the multiplier reel reveals x2, x5 or x10, all wins in that spin will be multiplied by the revealed multiplier before wins are paid.
Fortune Dragon Feature
During any spin, the Fortune Dragon Feature with 8 Fortune Spins may be randomly triggered while the reels are spinning.
During the Fortune Dragon Feature, the multiplier reel will be expanded and 2 or 3 multipliers will be awarded for every Fortune spin.
Maximum Win
The maximum win amount is 2,500x of the bet amount.
If the total win in the main game or the Fortune Dragon Feature reaches 2,500x of the bet amount, the spin ends.
Sure Win Bet
The Sure Win Bet option can be activated for each spin which increases the bet by 5x.
Activating the Sure Win Bet will guarantee a win for every spin (excluding Fortune Spin).
Current Bet Size and Bet Level will not be affected by the Sure Win Bet.
The Sure Win Bet option can be activated and deactivated during the main game.

Additional Information
The game will automatically return to its initial state after being idle for 24 hours. Progress and status of any collection games, unfinished free games, and unfinished feature games etc will no longer be retained.
Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
In the event of disputes over game results, all game results that are shown in the game history page are absolute and final.
